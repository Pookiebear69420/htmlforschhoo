AI Usefulness

A Personal Charter for Using AI Responsibly in School
AI is my helper — not my brain.

How I Use AI Responsibly

  • Writing Support: Brainstorm ideas, improve grammar, and clarify my own writing.
  • Time Management: Build study schedules and break large assignments into steps.
  • Communication: Draft respectful emails to teachers.
  • Future Planning: Explore careers, skills, and goals.

The 5 AI Rules I Live By

  • AI is technology, not a human. It suggests — I decide.
  • Think critically. I question every answer.
  • Verify information. I double-check facts.
  • Protect privacy. No personal data shared.
  • Enhance, don’t replace. Learning comes first.

What I Will NOT Do

  • Submit AI work as my own
  • Use AI on tests without permission
  • Let AI think for me
  • Use AI to cheat

My AI Pledge

I will use AI as a tool, not a shortcut.
I will think, question, and learn.
I will respect my teachers and my future.
I will become smarter — not lazier.
Responsible AI starts with Parker.
