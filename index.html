A Personal Charter for Using AI Responsibly in School
AI is my helper.

How I Use AI Responsibly

  • Writing Support: Brainstorm ideas, improve grammar, and clarify my own writing.
  • Time Management: Build study schedules and break big assignments into manageable steps.
  • Communication: Draft respectful emails to teachers for recommendations, questions, or help.
  • Future Planning: Explore careers, skills, and goals for life after school.

The 5 AI Rules I Live By

  • AI is technology, not a human. It gives suggestions, I make decisions.
  • Critically evaluate responses. I don't trust answers without thinking them through.
  • If something feels off, investigate. I double-check facts and sources.
  • Keep private information private. No passwords, grades, or personal data shared with AI.
  • Use AI to boost my talents, not replace them. My brain does the real work.

What I Will NOT Do

  • Copy AI answers and submit them as my own work
  • Use AI during tests or quizzes without permission
  • Let AI do all my thinking
  • Use AI to cheat or avoid learning

My AI Pledge

I will use AI as a tool, not a shortcut.
I will think first, question second, and submit work I understand.
